uw_supplier_data040219.csv = monthly water use data downloaded from State Water Resources Control Board on 5/28/2019 and filtered to only the North Coast region
	Data source: https://data.ca.gov/dataset/drinking-water-public-water-system-operations-monthly-water-production-and-conservation
	Important fields are:
		- Supplier_Name = look for Healdsburg (closest city to Navarro)
		- Reporting_Month = month of data
		- R_GPCD_Reported = reported residential use, gallons per capita per day
		- R_GPCD_Calculated = estimated residential use, gallons per capita per day
